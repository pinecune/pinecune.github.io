This McCree Messenger app was built by and for mid-day enthusiasts.

Some commands you can use to chat with the big man himself:

what time is it

I tell you if it's high noon or not.


where is it high noon

I tell you where it will be/has been high noon.


my utc timezone offset is ###

Replace ### with a two digit number to set or change your UTC timezone offset. Be sure to include + or -, and lead with a 0 for values less than 10 (eg: 05).

For example:

my utc timezone offset is -05

my utc timezone offset is +09

my utc timezone offset is +11

my utc timezone offset is +00


when will it be high noon

I tell you when it'll be high noon where you are.


forget my utc timezone offset

I forver the utc timezone offset that you told me.


help

Refers you to this webpage.
